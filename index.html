About Me

I’m a software engineer who cares about building things that are clear, reliable, and built to last. I like structure. I like when systems make sense. And I’m intentional about writing code that’s clean and maintainable.

I’ve worked on full-stack projects and enjoy taking messy or complex ideas and turning them into something simple and functional. I’m especially motivated by problems that require both logic and thoughtfulness — not just quick fixes.

I’m drawn to EdTech and HealthTech because access matters. Technology should make important systems easier to use and understand, not more confusing. I want to build tools that quietly support real people in meaningful ways.

Long term, I’m interested in growing deeper into backend and platform work — focusing on the parts of a system that keep everything stable, secure, and running smoothly.
